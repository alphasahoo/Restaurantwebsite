WHO WE ARE

The Punjabi Rasoi, established in 1999 was launched with a single vision; to serve the people of Bangalore with authentic Punjabi food. It is the preservation of the high standards of north Indian cuisine and the consistent delivery of this service for over 12 years that makes The Punjabi Rasoi unique. With three established branches across Bangalore, the Punjabi Rasoi ensures that its diners are transported to the mainlands of Punjab through every bite of their meal.
